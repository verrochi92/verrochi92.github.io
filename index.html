The Axolotl Project

This project I did as my capstone at UMass. Our goal was to create software that could be used to take measurements from scans of Axolotls to help the McCusker lab study their regenerative properties. We used two Javascript libraries, OpenSeadragon and Fabric.js. This was a team project, but I wrote the majority of the code.
